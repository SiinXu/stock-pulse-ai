# ADR-NNN: Decision Title

- Status: `Proposed`
- Decision date: YYYY-MM-DD
- Decision owners: Name or role
- References: Issue, PR, code, contract, or evidence links

## Context

Describe the concrete problem, existing constraints, and evidence that make a
durable decision necessary. Distinguish verified facts from assumptions.

## Decision

State the chosen boundary or policy precisely. Include important exclusions so
the ADR cannot be read as authorization for a broader change.

## Consequences

Record both benefits and costs. Include compatibility limits, operational risks,
and follow-up obligations when they are part of the decision.
